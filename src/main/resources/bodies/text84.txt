Become a Member Sound bars Buying Guide As TVs slim down, the quality of their sound often follows suit. If you want richer, fuller sound, a sound bar is an easier and less expensive fix than a complete add-on component system. A sound bar has several speakers and the electronics to power them in one thin module you connect to the TV—no need for a receiver. Some have a separate subwoofer, usually wireless, and a few have rear speakers.
